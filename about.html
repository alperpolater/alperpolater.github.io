About Me

I am Alper Polater, a Mechanical Engineer specializing in project-based design and technical drawing. My experience spans various industries, including food machinery, metal production, and industrial piping. I have held positions as a Design Engineer, Production Engineer, and Site Engineer, where I contributed to numerous complex projects.

I graduated with a degree in Mechanical Engineering from Manisa Celal Bayar University and have certifications in SolidWorks, Welding Supervision, and Proficiency in Engineering.
